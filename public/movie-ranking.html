Top Movies of all time according to Srinivas

My best 3 Movies.

Baahubali : The Beginning

This an epic film which was directed by the legendary director S S Rajamouli. Starring Rebel Star Prabhas as the protagonist playing the role as Mahendra Baahubali as well as Amarendra Baahubali. This movie showcases how an elder brother of an actually capable king is ready to even betray and kill his own mother to get the throne in the greed for power. So sit back and enjoy the show to the fullest.

Em Maaya Chesava

This movie is one of my favs in the genre of romance. Starring Naga Chaitanya and Samantha. This has been always been a go to romance drama for the young generation in the time the movie was released and i can assure it still is!!
